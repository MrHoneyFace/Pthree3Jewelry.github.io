About Pthree3 Handmade Jewelry

Let not steadfast love and faithfulness forsake you; bind them around your neck, write them on the tablet of your heart. Proverbs 3:3

Pthree3 was created by a Christ-following wife and mother. The beauty and truth in each of God's many names found throughout the Bible inspired her to create jewelry showcasing His nature. The Lord is love and faithfulness personified, and Pthree3 has taken the directive given in Proverbs 3:3 literally, to help you bind the Lord and His names around your neck. I pray that wearing the necklace as you go throughout your day will remind you of the personal significance of God's name that you have chosen to wear.

May the Lord write Himself on the tablet of your heart, brothers and sisters.

Placing an Order

Welcome to Pthree3 Handmade Jewelry! We are thrilled that you are interested in our artisan-made jewelry. Placing an order is simple. Just browse through our collection, select the items you love, and proceed to checkout. We provide secure, convenient payment through PayPal and offer free shipping to ensure your order reaches you promptly and with no hidden costs.

Custom Orders

If you are seeking a specific name of God on a necklace not currently available in our shop, please contact us at Pthree3Jewelry@gmail.com to request your custom jewelry. We are happy to work with you to create a necklace that you love!

Shipping

Every order always includes free shipping! Necklaces will ship as soon as possible, usually within one week of ordering.

Return/Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing our company. We take pride in the quality of our products and services. Due to the handcrafted, customized nature of our business, we do not accept returns or refunds. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in this matter. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us.
